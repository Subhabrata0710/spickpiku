Welcome to Monsoon CME 2026

A premier academic event bringing together leading experts in pediatric intensive care

We are pleased to invite you to the Monsoon Conference of Pediatric Intensive Care to be held on 14th June 2026 at The Park Kolkata, with several pre-conference workshops on 13th June 2026.

The conference is jointly hosted by The Society of Pediatric Intensive Care Kolkata, Intensive Care Chapter of Indian Academy of Pediatrics, and West Bengal Academy of Pediatrics.

Simultaneous 5 workshops on very demanding topics will be conducted at different institutions. Delegates will get ample opportunity for hands-on training and clear their doubts from the leading experts and academicians in the field.

To encourage research, we will have oral and poster presentations by the residents. We expect this will be of huge interest for residents.
