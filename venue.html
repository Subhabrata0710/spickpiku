The Park, Kolkata

Located in the heart of the city on historic Park Street, The Park is one of Kolkata's most iconic luxury hotels. Known for its contemporary style, warm hospitality, and vibrant atmosphere, it offers world-class conference facilities, upscale dining, and a chic ambience ideal for both business and leisure.

Its central location makes it easily accessible and a perfect venue for premier academic events like the Monsoon Conference of Pediatric Intensive Care.

Address: 17 Park Street, Kolkata 700016, West Bengal, India
