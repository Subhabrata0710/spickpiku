www.spickpicu.org | spickpicu@gmail.com | 9830367423Dear Friends & Colleagues, 
We are pleased to invite you to the Monsoon CME of Pediatric Intensive
Care to be held on 14th June 2026 at The Park Kolkata, with several Pre-
Conference workshops to be held on the 13th June 2026. The conference is
jointly hosted by The Society of Pediatric Intensive Care Kolkata, Intensive
Care Chapter of Indian Academy of Pediatrics and West Bengal Academy of
Pediatrics. 
Keeping in mind the need of pediatric cardiac intensive care, there will be
parallel session on basic issues in this field to sensitize the residents and
pediatricians. There will be equal emphasis on advanced intensive care for
practicing intensivists and basic intensive/emergency care for the practicing
pediatrician. 
Nurses are the backbone and driving force for PICU. We received
overwhelming response from the participants of nursing workshop last year.
We are conducting Paediatric Critical Care Nursing Workshop. Encourage
the nurses to join in the engaging workshop. 
Simultaneous 4 workshops on very demanding topics will be conducted at
different institutions. Delegates will get ample opportunity for hands on
training and clear their doubts from the leading experts and academician in
the field.
To encourage research, we will have oral and poster presentation by the
residents. We expect this will be of huge interest for residents.
Team Monsoon CME of Pediatric Intensive Care, Kolkata
